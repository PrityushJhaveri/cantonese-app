           1. ngǒs 我                Pn:  I;                                         
                                          me                                        
                                         [Note that there is no case distinction for personal pronouns
                                         in Cantonese.]                             
           2. něi5 你/您              Pn:  you (singular)                             
                                         [Note that some people in Hong Kong will say "lějs"
                                         instead of "nějs".]                        
                                                                                    
           3. kěois 佢               Pn:  he; him; she; her; it                      
                                         [There is no gender distinction between he, she or it in
                                         Cantonese.]                                
                                         [Note that some HK people will say "hěoj5" instead of
                                          "kěoj5"]                                  
           4. hóu²                  Adj: good; well; fine; okay                     
                                                                                    
           5. něi³ hóu² 你好          SE:  (lit. you good) Hello, Hi, Good day.       
                                         [Note that no verb-to-be is needed in a general adjectival
                                         predicate sentence.]                       
                                                                                    
           6. hāis 係                V:   be (is, am, are; was, were)                
              (N, + hài® +N2)            [used for all cases, numbers, tenses, etc.]
                                         [Note that there is no conjugation for verbs in Cantonese.]
                                                                                    
           7. Hõeng¹ Góng2 Dãai Hōk PN:  The University of Hong Kong                
              香港大學                       ("Góng2 Dãai®"i s used for the abbreviation.)

              (N, + gẻ³ + N2)            to 's in English]                          
                                                                                    
           9. hõk® sāang¹ 學生        N:   student(s)                                 
           10. giu³ 매               V:   to be called, named; known as; to call     
              (N,

                                         name [Note that Chinese surnames always come before
                                         Chinese given names.]

                                         to customers or visitors]                  
           13. síu² zé² 小姐          N:   Miss; young lady
